EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into as of February 15, 2026,
between StartupXYZ Inc. ("Company") and Jane Smith ("Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee is hired as Senior Software Engineer. Employee agrees to devote full
business time and attention to the Company and shall not engage in other employment
without prior written consent.

2. COMPENSATION
Base salary: $120,000 per year, paid bi-weekly.
Annual bonus: discretionary, at Company sole determination.
Stock options: 10,000 options vesting over 4 years with 1-year cliff.

3. BENEFITS
Employee is eligible for health insurance, dental, 15 days PTO per year,
and participation in 401(k) plan after 90 days.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Employee agrees that all inventions, improvements, and developments conceived
during employment are the exclusive property of the Company, including inventions
created outside of working hours if they relate to the Company business.

5. NON-COMPETE
For 12 months following termination, Employee shall not work for any direct
competitor within the United States. A list of competitors is attached as Exhibit A.

6. NON-SOLICITATION
For 18 months following termination, Employee shall not solicit Company employees
or customers.

7. AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT
Employment is at-will. Company may terminate at any time with or without cause,
with 2 weeks severance if terminated without cause after 1 year of service.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee shall maintain confidentiality of all Company information during and for
3 years after employment.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by California law. Disputes resolved in San Francisco courts.
