FREELANCE SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into as of January 1, 2026,
between TechCorp Inc. ("Client") and the individual identified below ("Contractor").

1. SERVICES
Contractor agrees to provide software development services as requested by Client.
Specific deliverables will be defined in separate Statements of Work.

2. PAYMENT
Client shall pay Contractor $85 per hour. Invoices are due within 60 days of receipt.
Late payments shall incur a penalty of 0.5% per month.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product, inventions, and improvements created by Contractor during this Agreement,
WHETHER OR NOT during working hours or using Client equipment, shall be the sole property
of Client. This assignment includes any work created on Contractor personal time that
relates in any way to Client business or industry.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Contractor agrees to keep all Client information confidential. This obligation shall
survive termination INDEFINITELY and without limitation.

5. NON-COMPETE
During the term and for THREE (3) YEARS following termination, Contractor shall not
engage in any business activity that competes with Client ANYWHERE IN THE WORLD.

6. TERMINATION
Client may terminate this Agreement with TWO (2) days written notice.
Contractor may terminate with 30 days written notice.

7. LIABILITY
Contractor liability for any claims shall be UNLIMITED.
Client liability shall be limited to amounts paid in the prior 30 days.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Delaware, USA.

9. AUTO-RENEWAL
This Agreement automatically renews unless notice is given at least SEVEN (7) days
before the end of the current term.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration with costs borne entirely by Contractor.
