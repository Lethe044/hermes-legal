FREELANCE SERVICE AGREEMENT — VERSION 2 (REVISED)

This Agreement is entered into as of March 1, 2026,
between TechCorp Inc. ("Client") and the individual identified below ("Contractor").

1. SERVICES
Contractor agrees to provide software development services as requested by Client.
Specific deliverables will be defined in separate Statements of Work.

2. PAYMENT
Client shall pay Contractor $95 per hour (increased from $85). Invoices are due within
30 days of receipt (improved from 60 days).
Late payments shall incur a penalty of 1.5% per month.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product created by Contractor DURING WORKING HOURS using Client equipment
shall be the property of Client. Work created on Contractor personal time is excluded.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Contractor agrees to keep all Client information confidential for a period of THREE (3)
YEARS following termination of this Agreement.

5. NON-COMPETE
During the term and for ONE (1) YEAR following termination, Contractor shall not engage
in business activities that directly compete with Client within the United States only.

6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with THIRTY (30) days written notice.

7. LIABILITY
Each party's liability shall be limited to the total fees paid in the prior SIX (6) months.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of California, USA.

9. AUTO-RENEWAL
This Agreement shall automatically renew unless either party provides written notice
at least SIXTY (60) days before the end of the current term.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes shall be resolved through mediation first, then arbitration if needed.
Costs shall be shared equally between both parties.
