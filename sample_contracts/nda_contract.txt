MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual NDA is entered into as of March 1, 2026,
between Alpha Ventures LLC ("Party A") and Beta Solutions Ltd ("Party B").

1. PURPOSE
The parties wish to explore a potential business partnership and may need to share
confidential information with each other.

2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
Confidential Information means any and all information disclosed by either party,
including but not limited to business plans, financial data, technical specifications,
customer lists, pricing, and any other information marked as confidential or which
a reasonable person would consider confidential given the circumstances.

3. OBLIGATIONS
Each party agrees to: (a) hold the other party Confidential Information in strict
confidence; (b) not disclose such information to third parties without prior written
consent; (c) use the information solely for evaluating the potential partnership.

4. TERM AND SURVIVAL
This Agreement shall remain in effect for two (2) years from the date of execution.
Confidentiality obligations for trade secrets shall survive indefinitely.
For all other information, obligations survive for 3 years post-termination.

5. EXCLUSIONS
Information is not confidential if: (a) it is or becomes publicly known through no
breach of this Agreement; (b) it was rightfully known before disclosure;
(c) it is independently developed without use of confidential information.

6. RETURN OF INFORMATION
Upon request or termination, each party shall promptly return or destroy all
confidential materials.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales.
Disputes shall be resolved through mediation before litigation.

8. REMEDIES
Each party acknowledges that breach may cause irreparable harm and that injunctive
relief may be sought without posting bond.
